<Ingredients>
|                                      |               |
| :----------------------------------- | ------------: |
| Button Mushrooms                     | 20 medium/large |
| Gournay Cheese (like garlic & fine herbs Boursin) | 100g        |
</Ingredients>

<Instructions>
1. Wash the **mushrooms** and pull off the stems.
2. Stuff the inside of the mushrooms with **Gournay cheese** and set on a baking tray.
3. Bake at 220Â° for about 15 minutes or until the mushrooms are cooked through and the Gournay cheese begins to brown on top.
4. Enjoy!
</Instructions>

<InfoBox title="Tip!">
Make this super simple recipe as an **appetizer** or a side.  

Use the mushroom **stems** in a separate recipe or roast them alongside the stuffed mushroom, do not waste them!
</InfoBox>
